## Common Enemy (Buffalo Farm) Remix

**Status:** Open — riff translation confirmed, test in DAW pending

**Context:**
Heavy track. Vocals only from original — building everything else around them. Guitar riff motif kernel: G-F#-A-Bb (translated from C major original). Key: G harmonic minor. F#→G chromatic pull is the spine of the motif — preserves the tension from the original, hits harder in minor context. Bb (b3) carries the darkness. Chug breathes slightly against the vocal rhythmically — runs parallel, not locked. Emotional texture: grief that became anger. Controlled, coiled, not chaotic. The dark side earns its place.
