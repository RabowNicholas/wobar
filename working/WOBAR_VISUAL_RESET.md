# WOBAR VISUAL DESIGN SYSTEM — RESET

Nick stepped away from visualizers — output read competent but generic. This doc
diagnoses why against the world v0.6, then builds the replacement.

**The finding, v0.2 (2026-07-14, session 2) — supersedes the v0.1 line below.**
The gap was never craft, and never palette. It was that **no committed look
existed to execute.** The vault held a world described entirely in words — Portal,
Void, Mirror, Wanderer, the Ether, two broken circles — and zero pixels that world
committed to. "A tunnel inward" is a *camera move*, not a look; it says where the
camera goes and nothing about what you see. That is why material kept getting
"discovered in-build" and kept not landing: there was nothing to discover *toward*.

The spine in §3 fixes this by supplying the missing thing — not a style, a
**mechanism**: the corridor is the *artifact* of a perception failing to render a
higher-dimensional signal. Every rule that was previously a preference (never
arrive, no pure white, the broken mark, OBSCURA's stillness) is now a consequence
of that one fact.

*v0.1 line, kept for the record: "the palette and desaturation discipline are strong
and world-aligned; the moving-portal FORM vocabulary is the generic-drift culprit
and now contradicts where the world went. The reset is a geometry swap, not a
repaint." — half right. The palette call held; the diagnosis of the tunnel did not.
The tunnel was correct all along; its stated reason was wrong, and the reason is
what generates form.*

## 1. THE AUDIT — old system vs world v0.6

Old system = `WOBAR_TD_REFERENCE §3/§4` + `WOBAR_GLSL_PATTERNS` + `WOBAR_OBSCURA` +
`WOBAR_BRAND` look + shipped pieces (down_bad_3stack, crease_sa, crease_veil).

### Contradictions (old fights world)
- **C1 — The portal-motion spine.** 8 of 10 GLSL shaders are portal/spiral/tunnel/
  radial-burst. World killed "swirly-portal" (web home) and matured toward
  stillness (Encounter "held not chaotic"; OBSCURA geometry "is still, does not

  independent of the world. **OPEN** — provisional: gloss only at mirror-moments.
- **C3 — "Desaturated *psychedelic*" framing** pulls organic/ceremonial vs a cold
  terminal register. **Resolved: not a defect — the visualizer is its own organ
  (ceremonial), separate from the web-home terminal (§7.5). Kept.**

### Gaps (world demands what old system never specified)
- **G1 — No house constant.** Per-act primitives + *suggested* palettes, zero
  through-line. Every piece reinvents from zero. The generic-drift root.
- **G2 — The mark is absent from the visual system.** No mark/sigil in BRAND or TD
  spec. World locked **two broken circles**. Nothing threads it through.
- **G3 — Convergence has no visual grammar.** World §3: a finished piece holds all

- **G4 — Mirror-as-thesis under-built.** World makes the Mirror central at every

- **G5 — The tightrope has no visual guardrail.** Psychological-surface /
  cosmology-submerged / no-full-woo is doctrine, never written as a visual rule.

### Generic drift (the dissatisfaction root)
- **D1 — Portal/spiral/tunnel/burst = the most generic EDM-visualizer vocabulary
  that exists.** Strip the palette and the 10 shaders are stock Milkdrop/Resolume.
  The desaturated palette is the ONLY thing keeping them off-the-shelf.
- **D2 — House style carried by color alone.** Nothing structural is proprietary.
  A design system needs a proprietary FORM, not just a proprietary color list.
- **D3 — CA + bloom + grade + grain** = the universal "make it look pro" stack.
  Competent, anywhere, not WOBAR.

### Still-good (keep, carry into the reset)
- **K1 — Desaturation law** (muted 30–40%, no pure white, neon ban list). Strongest
  asset. Keep verbatim.
- **K2 — The palette.** Rich, distinctive, aligned. Keep; tighten per-act from
  *suggestion* → *rule*.
- **K3 — Imperfect-mirror rule** (85–90%; "100% = decorative not confrontational").
  Genuinely WOBAR. Elevate Act 3 trick → house law.
- **K4 — OBSCURA's whole vocabulary** — still geometry, "reflection geometry,
  symbols that complete only when mirrored," faceless "form without definition,
  presence without identity," negative space as primary form, camera-obscura
  aperture, "the gap between observer and the image looking back." The most
  world-aligned material in the entire old system, locked since April, barely used.
  It IS the two-broken-circles mark and the anti-swirl resolution, pre-written.
  **This is the seed of the new world.**
- **K5 — Abstraction stance** (zero figures). Aligns faceless + anti-cheese. Keep,
  make explicit.

## 3. THE NEW WORLD — the spine (v0.2 — LOCKED 2026-07-14, session 2)

> **v0.1 superseded.** The first pass had the constant right (a tunnel inward) but
> the *reason* wrong, and the reason is what generates form. v0.1 treated the
> tunnel as a real passage toward a real destination, and left material to be
> "discovered in-build" — which is why five pieces produced five v0s. The ground
> truth below came from Nick in session, unprompted, from lived experience.

### THE GROUND TRUTH — the corridor is the artifact, not the territory

**The corridor is not a place. It is what 3D perception outputs when handed a
higher-dimensional signal it cannot render.** (Nick, verbatim: *"the corridor is my
limited understanding of higher dimensions trying to resolve it."*)

It is the **error**, not the territory. The lowest-error approximation your
perception can produce, and it is not enough. Everything below falls out of this
one fact.

**Why this matters more than the tunnel itself:** it converts every rule in the
system from a style preference into a mechanism. Rules you follow on faith produce
generic work. Mechanisms produce form.

### WHAT FALLS OUT — four consequences, all load-bearing

**1. Non-arrival is structural, not stylistic.**
You cannot reach the void down the corridor **because the corridor does not lead
there** — it is a mis-rendering *of* there. The void isn't at the end; the corridor
is a bad picture of it. Walking is the wrong verb. Arrival is not forbidden by
taste — it is *impossible by construction*.

Nick, in session: *"we can't arrive there… the music is always about the journey
into that space. That space often comes in the silent moments that live after."*
Which this explains exactly: **when perception stops trying, the artifact
dissolves, and what's left is the thing.** The corridor was in the way the whole
time. The piece can never deliver the void; the silence after the piece can.

**2. The drop is maximum failure — not arrival.**
This resolves the system's hardest conflict. Every EDM instinct says drop = arrive
= reveal; the world forbids arrival. Under the artifact model the drop is **the
resolve failing hardest** — maximum pressure on a perception that cannot cope.
Not a payoff. A collapse.

**3. The corridor is the fallback — the safe state.**
Perception *retreats* to corridor. It is what your mind has when it has nothing.
The uncanny moments are where it can't retreat fast enough.

**4. The corridor is STABLE at the open** *(Nick, locked 2026-07-14: "the corridor
is stable at the beginning. this allows the floor to fall out.")*
It is solid, hypnotic, believable — you buy it. That belief is what makes the
collapse cost something. A corridor visibly straining from frame one has no floor
to drop.

### THE PIECE'S SHAPE — the loop

```
corridor (stable, coping, hypnotic)
   → pressure
   → the floor falls out — corridor briefly stops being a corridor,
     becomes something the eye cannot hold
   → snaps back to corridor (it's all perception has)
   → repeat, deeper
   → never resolves · never arrives
```

**This loop IS the hypnosis.** Not repetition-because-hypnotic-reads-as-a-vibe —
hypnotic because **the resolve keeps failing and cannot stop trying.** Reach,
almost, collapse, reach again. That is what it feels like to think about four
dimensions, and it is the mechanic (Nick: *"almost like what it feels like to think
about four dimensions… us without the construct of time"* — WORLD §4, Axis 1).

**The design brief, stated:** WORLD §7 already established the mechanic for
language — a higher-dimensional message cannot be spoken linearly, so **poetry is
the translation layer**, the one form that holds paradox and non-linearity. The
visual system needs the same property one medium over: **an image that holds
something which will not resolve.** Don't depict the higher dimension. Depict
failing to.

### THE CONSTANT — every visualizer

- **A corridor. Moving through. Never arriving.** Full-frame, hypnotic, slow.
- **Stable at the open** → destabilizes under pressure → snaps back → never resolves.
- **The end is implied, never delivered.** No arrival shot, no widening into a
  room, no reveal, no light you reach.
- **Still and silent** is the register at the far end (the void is still and
  silent) — the piece approaches stillness, not climax.
- **Desaturated** (K1 — including no pure white), grain / degraded / analog-dirty,
  **imperfect mirror** 85–90% (K3), abstraction-only (K5).
- **Impossible geometry is a symptom, not a style.** The corridor connects wrong
  because you are seeing more of it than 3D permits.

### THE COLLAPSE — superposition (PROVEN in build, 2026-07-14)

See `prototypes/corridor/` — approved mechanic, dead ends, and the five failures
that produced it. Nick: *"this is closer, this concept is approaching what i see"*
and *"i actually like this more than my original idea."*

**N copies of the SAME corridor, superposed — never combined.** Each raymarched
independently, composited by weight. They meet in the image, never in the field.

**NO SDF BOOLEANS. EVER.** `min` / `max` / `smin` all take N fields and return
**one surface** — resolving is their entire job, and resolving is exactly what must
not happen. Five versions failed on this single point, and no parameter tweak could
ever have fixed it. Union carves a shared volume into one connected space — a cave —
which is *arrival*, and banned.

**The collapse is the weights equalizing.** Corridor 0 (the reading perception
committed to) sits at weight 1 always. The others are **always there at weight 0** —
unrendered, not absent. As the resolve fails their weights rise until all sit at
1/N and none is the corridor. **Nothing is created. Perception stops being able to
prefer one.** This is WORLD §4's ***uncollapsed*** — "every moment at once,
uncollapsed" — built. And §4's "any one face would be a lie, one time-slice frozen":
any one corridor is a lie; the truth is all of them at once.

Corollary: the snap-back is perception **re-committing** — it cannot operate
uncommitted, so it grabs a reading and calls it the corridor again. "Deeper each
time" = re-committing gets harder.

**Symmetry, not asymmetry** (Nick, locked). Arbitrary angles produce soup. Symmetric
offsets **interfere**, and interference is legible — it reads as architecture.
Escher's impossible geometry is highly symmetric; symmetry is what makes a thing
readable *as impossible* rather than as mush. No conflict with the anti-kaleidoscope
rule: the *arrangement* is clean symmetry while the *mirror* stays imperfect (K3).

**The offset is the geometry** — the primary artistic knob, ahead of the collapse.

**Current build: 4, same corridor, 4-fold symmetric, parallel.**

### THE HINT — CLOSED. The convergence point.

Because the copies stay **parallel**, they share **one vanishing point**. They
coincide exactly at the convergence and diverge everywhere else: **sharp at the
centre, multiplied at the edges.**

**The four only agree at the point you can't reach.** Everything visible is
uncertain; the only certain thing is the one you never get to. Four contradictory
readings of the path, one unanimous destination.

**That is the hint** — and it is not a light or a door bolted onto the end. It is
just where the readings agree. Nothing added. Emergent from the construction, not
designed. *(Nick's earlier illustration of a hint — a corridor ending in two bright
white lights behind a door — stays killed as a piece; this supersedes it as the
formal rule.)*

⚠️ **Never give the copies different recession axes.** Different axes → different
vanishing points → the convergence is destroyed. Claude proposed exactly this and
was about to ship it; Nick caught it. **The parallelism is load-bearing.**

### THE VARIABLE — the lane

WORLD §2: *"the map is fixed; the terrain within it is infinite. Each visualizer is
a different region of the same interior."* The corridor + the failure-loop is the
fixed map. **The lane is the terrain — open-ended, and more will come** (Nick:
*"those are four examples… even more could come of it"*).

Lanes as Nick has actually experienced them:
- **Impossible geometry / infinite dimension** — non-euclidean; folds back, repeats
  wrong, connects where it can't. *Current direction — the strongest moat; almost
  nobody in this lane does true impossible space, because tunnels are easy and
  impossible space is not.*
- **Organic**
- **Geometrical**
- **This dimension** — real architecture, actual corridors. LIVE as a lane, but
  **deprioritized 2026-07-14** — Nick pulled toward hypnotic / psychedelic /
  abstract. (The hospital-hallway piece was killed; the image survives only as the
  mark evidence above.)

**Material rule (replaces the material↔act affinity table):** stop designing
textures. **The corridor is the shape perception forced the signal into; the
material is what leaked through the forcing** — the parts that wouldn't compress.
Detail present because the real thing has structure, rendered wrong.

### ANTI-TARGETS — what kills it (new, this session)

- **The rotating tesseract / hypercube wireframe.** THE 4D cliché. It's boring
  *because it resolves*. Yours must never resolve — that's the entire point.
- **Fractal zooms, DMT-entity kaleidoscopes, sacred geometry, mandalas, third
  eyes.** Full-woo — banned by WORLD §0.2 (the tightrope), not by taste.
- **Cosmic / galaxy / floating / dissolution / ascension.** Banned by WORLD §6.2
  (**NO ESCAPE** — "a way *through*, never a way *out*"). Note: "the void" means
  *cosmos/infinite* in every other EDM visual; in this world it is a **black
  chamber** — a room, enclosed, walls. That one word is most of the un-generic.
- **Arrival / reveal / payoff / the light you reach.** Banned by non-arrival.
- **Liminal-space / backrooms dread.** The specific failure mode of the "this
  dimension" lane, and currently one of the most exhausted aesthetics online.
  Separators if that lane is ever run: it is **not horror** (backrooms is about
  *dread*; this is about *approach* — it wants you, it isn't threatening you),
  it's still and silent not jumpscare-and-flicker, desaturated not piss-yellow,
  and it never builds to anything.
- **Glitch-as-effect.** The corridor failing must not read as a digital artifact
  or datamosh. It's a *perceptual* failure, not a codec one.

### OBSCURA — a possible reconciliation (C1 still OPEN — Claude-derived, unruled)

OBSCURA (locked 2026-04-05) says WOBAR geometry moves — spirals, tunnels, portals —
while **OBSCURA geometry is still: "It does not pull you through anything. It holds
the shape of what is already there."** v0.1 read this as contradiction C1.

**Proposed:** there is no corridor in OBSCURA because there is no failed perception
there. The corridor is the artifact; OBSCURA is *"what exists inside the mirror,
behind the reflection"* — the other side, where the artifact has dissolved. Wobar
takes you through (the corridor / the failure); OBSCURA receives you on the other
side (still, silent — the void itself).

### WORKING MODE — look-dev in WebGL, finish in TD (established 2026-07-14)

**Prototype in a WebGL sketch. Port to `glslTOP` once the geometry reads.** A sketch
iterates in seconds and puts judgment *minutes* into a session instead of hours —
five prior WOBAR pieces were each built ground-up in TD and each landed at v0. The
approved collapse mechanic took six versions in one sitting; in TD that is a week.
Sketches live in `prototypes/corridor/`.

**TD is the right finisher and this was re-litigated 2026-07-14.** Claude's earlier
anti-TD argument (Houdini for real light/PBR/camera) **died with the photoreal
lane** — an impossible corridor has no PBR, no GI, no lit set. Impossible geometry
is a raymarching problem, i.e. a fragment-shader problem, and TD is a fragment-shader
host with the best audio plumbing available plus a node post-chain. **TD was never
the bottleneck — the missing spine was.** The $600 is an *output* question (res cap +
h264), cleanly separable: develop at 720×1280 on NC, buy when shipping.
