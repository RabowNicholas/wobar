# WOBAR VISUAL DESIGN SYSTEM — THE WIREFRAME-VOID WORLD

> **Derives from `[[WOBAR_GRAMMAR]]`.** The visual is one surface of one world (with
> sound and word). The wireframe-void spine below is the grammar's laws rendered in
> image — Law 0 (muted not vivid; abstract, instructs nothing; meaning emerges), void +
> one element (black + one wireframe form), the element is a mirror, the three-version
> render layers. If this file and the grammar ever disagree, reconcile deliberately.

Nick stepped away from visualizers — output read competent but generic. v0.1/v0.2
diagnosed *why* (below, §1 — still valid), then tried to fix it with the **corridor
spine**: the visual as a *rendering of a concept* (perception failing to render a
higher dimension; non-arrival, drop-as-failure, the stable→collapse loop,
superposition). **That is retired.**

**Why it was retired (Nick, 2026-07-21):**
1. **You cannot render the void — the void is empty.** The corridor tried to depict
   the destination directly. WORLD §2 says the chamber is empty black. There is
   nothing there to render.
2. **Non-resolving impossible geometry doesn't work** — unbuildable, and unwatchable.
3. It was concept-first and heavy, which is exactly what kept producing v0s.

**The replacement is not a new look — it is a new *kind* of thing.** Not a rendering
of a concept. **A world with its own physics.** You don't build the void; you build
the one thing in it, and let the emptiness stay empty.

## 2. THE SPINE — the wireframe-void world (LOCKED 2026-07-21)

**The physics of the world:**

- **Low-poly wireframe forms in true black.** Edges, not surfaces. Faceted, not smooth.
- **Muted-psychedelic** (K1). Vector *structure*, WOBAR *desaturation* — never the
  bright-line vector-cheese of synthwave/outrun.
- **Naturally empty.** The frame is mostly black — because the void *is* empty (WORLD
  §2). You do not fill it.
- **One contrasting element that holds attention.** Empty + a single focal form. That
  form is *what you meet in the chamber* (WORLD §2). It is a **mirror** — abstract, so
  you project onto it — at any depth, **up to and including the center itself** (the
  §8 wide strike, 2026-07-21: the center may be *shown*, never *explained*).

**Coherence comes from the shared world, not from a repeated mechanic.** Every piece
is recognizably WOBAR because it lives in the same world — the same black, the same
wireframe, the same restraint — not because it re-runs a formula.

**Simplicity is doctrine, not compromise.** The tightrope (WORLD §0.2) says the
surface stays simple while the cosmology stays submerged. A clean wireframe form
breathing in black *keeps the cosmology submerged by construction.* So the instinct
to simplify and let the music lead is the brand getting **more** correct, not less
ambitious. This is the visual guardrail G5 was missing.

## 3. THE BELIEFS BUILD IN — why the medium IS the brand

Most of these are not metaphors. The medium literally *is* the belief.

**Geography (WORLD §2):**
- **True black = the Void / Chamber.** Empty *is* the content. Where the walls are thin.
- **The one wireframe element = what you meet in the chamber.** The encounter.
- **Wireframe (edges, no surface) = the Portal goes inward.** You see *into* / *through*
  the form, never land on its skin. Looking-in is built into the medium.
- **Wandering = the slow breathing / drift** through the emptiness.

**The Entity (WORLD §4):**
- **Wireframe = all edges at once, front and back = "every moment at once,
  uncollapsed"** (Axis 1). Built into the medium — no superposition trick needed.
- **Low-poly = the blueprint = the source** (Axis 2, "the blueprint of the universe").
  Low-poly is a schematic; you render the source as its own wireframe schematic.
  **Low-poly is load-bearing, not convenience.**
- **No surface, no face, no single form = facelessness over-determined.**

**Doctrine (WORLD §6):**
- **IT'S ALL WITHIN YOU (§6.1):** you see the inside/structure, never the external skin.
- **NO ESCAPE (§6.2):** the void is a chamber you're *in* — heavy, submerged, enclosed —
  not a cosmos you float in. **This is why muted, not vivid, is doctrine:** vivid = the
  trip, the escape; muted = staying in the body.
- **INTENTION (§6.3):** the empty frame *demands* attention — the single element only
  means something because you look. The composition requires the viewer to complete it.
- **NO GURU / empower never instruct (§6.4, §8):** an abstract element instructs nothing.
  You project onto it. The center may be *shown*, never *handed to you as the answer*.
- **THE TIGHTROPE (§0.2, §6.7):** surface simple, cosmology submerged. Simplicity IS
  compliance.

## 4. THE ACTS — one world, five behaviors

Whole-brand: the same medium reads as every act. **The act lives in the behavior of
the single element + palette temperature + the layer mix (§5)** — not in a different
primitive per act.

- **Act 1 — RIFT:** element stable, whole, contained. The safe container. Cool/neutral,
  clean wireframe, minimal decay.
- **Act 2 — DESCENSION:** element breathes, hypnotic, sinking. It *persists* and pulses
  with the sub — never becomes a different form (EMO Act 2 law: "the form is the same
  form throughout"). Deep dubstep home. (First build: `kekkai_low_poly`.)
- **Act 3 — ENCOUNTER:** element hard to look at — dense, self-intersecting, tension in
  the wireframe, the decay layer rising. Held, organized, not chaotic (EMO Act 3).
- **Act 4 — RELEASE:** the discharge. Max chaos/energy — the one act where the element
  is allowed to break its own form-law. Palette can reach fullest (still muted-ceiling).
- **Act 5 — INTEGRATION:** settles, grounded, the layers balanced. Resolution permitted
  (resolving is no longer banned — 2026-07-21).

**Motion law (all acts):** one form, breathing with the sound. It does not transform
into a *different* shape (EMO Act 2). The one sanctioned loosening is Act 4's discharge.

## 5. THE TOOLBOX — techniques (not doctrine; reach for them per-piece)

**The three-version render layers (the convergence grammar, G3).** *Optional per
piece — a strong way to hit WORLD §3's "all three at once," not a required mechanic.*
- **wireframe = Future** (the blueprint, clean structure)
- **sub-displacement = Present** (the body/mass responding now)
- **phosphor-decay + grain = Wounded** (hauntology, the trailing damage)
- convergence = all three composited on the one element at once.

**Confirmed vector-void build recipe (from `kekkai_low_poly`, TD_BUILD_LOG 2026-07-18):**
- Form: `sphereSOP` (poly, low-res) → `noiseSOP` displace (sub-driven) → `wireframeMAT`
  (`wireframemode='tesselated'`). *No native icosphere in sphereSOP — a true geodesic
  low-poly needs a Blender import (still open).*
- Color: white wireframe × single muted color via `hsvadjustTOP hueoffset`; tempo-locked
  hue drift `(me.time.seconds*(Bpm/60)/Huebeats*360)%360`, **timer-only (audio dropped
  from color per Nick)**, muted-psych spectrum, cool-biased.
- Audio: sub → displace, energy → chaos (3-axis tumble + feedback swirl). Transient
  still unmapped.
- Post: HDR (ACES + subtle CA + vignette + 16mm grain + no-pure-white grade).
- Master-driven (`parentshortcut='Ctrl'`).

**Known gotchas:** `wireframeSOP` output can't be instanced directly — insert
`convertSOP totype='poly'` after it. `hsvadjustTOP hueoffset` has no effect on pure
white — tint the wireframe slightly first, then rotate hue.

## 6. THE LAWS — house rules + anti-targets

**House law (carried from the audit K-rules):**
- **K1 — Muted-psychedelic. No pure white. No neon.** The desaturation law.
- **K3 — Imperfect mirror 85–90%** wherever mirroring is used. 100% = decorative, not
  confrontational.
- **K5 — Abstraction only. Zero figures.** Faceless + anti-cheese.

**Anti-targets — what kills it:**
- **Synthwave / outrun / neon-grid.** THE vector-cheese vector. Vector-void is
  early-80s Battlezone/Elite register — desaturated, not glowing-magenta nostalgia.
  *This is the sharpest anti-target for the new world.*
- **Cosmic / galaxy / floating / dissolution / ascension.** Banned by NO ESCAPE (§6.2).
  "The void" here is a **black chamber** — a room, enclosed — never cosmos.
- **Full-woo:** sacred-geometry mandalas, third eyes, DMT-entity kaleidoscopes. Banned
  by the tightrope (§0.2), not by taste.
- **Vivid / high-saturation.** Reads as the trip/escape; violates muted-psych + NO ESCAPE.
- **The "make it pro" stack as the identity** (CA + bloom + grade + grain). Fine as
  finish; never the substance (D3).
- **Filling the frame.** Emptiness is the point. A busy frame is not this world.

**No longer banned (2026-07-21):** resolving, arrival, a form sitting whole. The
corridor's never-resolve law is retired. Forms may resolve; the center may be shown.

## 7. CLOSED THREADS + LEGACY

**G2 — the mark. CLOSED 2026-07-21.** The two-broken-circles mark has a home in the
wireframe-void world: line-based, it belongs. It can sit **at the center** — the
faceless face as the thing you reach — now that the center may be shown (§8 wide
strike). No longer forced; it fits the medium natively.

**C1 — the OBSCURA reconciliation. CLOSED 2026-07-21.** OBSCURA's still
reflection-geometry ("form without definition, presence without identity," negative
space as primary form, symbols that complete only when mirrored) *is* the
wireframe-void world's cousin — the same vocabulary (audit K4). OBSCURA is no longer a
contradiction to reconcile; it is a dialect of this world. (The prior corridor-era C1
proposal — OBSCURA as "the other side where the artifact dissolved" — died with the
corridor spine.)

**LEGACY — retired, not deleted:**
- The **corridor spine** (v0.1/v0.2): non-arrival, drop-as-failure, stable→collapse
  loop, superposition. Full text in git (`git show 1b7afaa:working/WOBAR_VISUAL_RESET.md`).
- `prototypes/corridor/` (the WebGL collapse sketches), `tunnel_oxidized`,
  `corridor_v1` (POP-3D lit tunnel), the **Corridor Build** loop. All superseded.
  Kept on disk as rig-building history; not part of the identity.
