# WOBAR CONTENT SYSTEM

## Philosophy
The process is the content. Not behind-the-scenes. Not reconstructed after the fact. The actual live experience of making the music — captured in the moment, lightly shaped, never polished into something it isn't.

The unit is not the post. The unit is the release. Every piece of music generates a complete content package built alongside it. Nothing gets posted until the package is finished. The song finishing is the trigger.

---

## The Two Buckets

### THE MIRROR
The music doing the work. Pure sonic and visual experience. No explanation needed. The viewer's reaction is visceral first, curious second.

Every Mirror post is an invitation to look into a reflection. Come find it live. Come find it on streaming.

**Formats:** Visualizer clips · Live set footage · Track releases · Full sets

### WHEN THE WALLS ARE THIN
You caught in the act of being Wobar. All three versions present. The walls thin enough that something real is happening and you captured it.

This is the inside of what The Mirror shows from the outside. Explicit about the why. Not serious about it.

**Formats:** Talking to camera · Written journal entries · Quote and idea carousels · Voice memos

**Shaping rule:** Trim it. Don't polish it. Raw enough to feel real, tight enough to land.

---

## Release Architecture

### Original Tracks
One per month. The primary release unit. Full content package built in parallel with the music across both buckets. Nothing posts until the package is complete. Once the trigger fires, the full package rolls out over 30 days at 3 post days per week.

### Portal EPs
5-track complete journeys mapping the full 5-Act Portal Framework — one track per act, Rift through Integration. Built from individual tracks already released.

### Flow State Sets and Live Mixes
Recorded live at Flow State events. Full content package. Flow State pushes the full YouTube set. Clips feed both pages. Mirror-heavy.

### Channels
Bedroom recordings. Something came through and got captured. No ceremony. Just more music in the world.

## Content Package Specs

### Original Track — Minimum Package

**The Mirror (minimum 6 assets)**
- 1 × Full visualizer (YouTube — full length, audio reactive)
- 2 × Short visualizer clips (TikTok/Instagram — 30–60 sec)
- 1 × 15 sec hook clip (TikTok/Instagram)
- 1 × Static cover art post (release day, all platforms)
- 1 × Track release (SoundCloud + Spotify)

**When The Walls Are Thin (minimum 4 assets)**
- 1 × Post-session journal entry (captured during production)
- 1 × Voice memo (captured in the moment)
- 1 × Talking to camera (the night it finished or a breakthrough session)
- 1 × Quote or idea carousel (from production sessions)

**Minimum total: 10 assets. The track does not post until all 10 exist.**

### Channels — Minimum Package
- 1 × SoundCloud upload
- 1 × Static Instagram post
- 1 × Flow State text to the sets sublist

## Posting System

**ANCHOR** — The package. 3 post days per week. Planned and deliberate. Defines Wobar.

**ORBIT** — Captured from real life. Studio, walks, drives, events, conversations about the framework. Unplanned. Light shaping or raw. Keeps daily cadence alive between anchor posts.

**REPOSTS AND CHANNELS** — Lowest lift. Flow State content, scene reposts, Channels drops. Fills gaps on days when nothing else is ready.

**Target:** 3x daily across TikTok and Instagram. Same asset posted to both counts as two posts.
