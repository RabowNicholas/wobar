# WOBAR GRAMMAR — ONE WORLD, THREE SURFACES

The world had three modality docs — visual, copy, sound — each deriving from WORLD
on its own, none from each other. Three siblings, no shared spine. So they read as
three worlds. This file is the spine. **Sound, image, and word are one grammar
rendered on three surfaces.** If the columns below line up, the world is one thing.

---

## LAW 0 — GROUND IT. LET DEPTH EMERGE. NEVER REACH.

The keystone. Every other law is a consequence of it.

**Ground the surface in something plain and real. Let the depth emerge from it. Never
reach for the depth directly.** The insider brings the meaning; the work never points
at it. The moment a piece reaches — for profundity, mood, mystique, hype, cleverness
— it collapses. Reaching is the sound of a small artist. Emerging is the sound of a
world.

Every Wobar cheese-vector is a form of reaching:
- **clever** (copy) · **vivid** (visual) · **woo** (cosmology) · **maximalist** (sound)
  · **mystique** (manufactured mood) · **meaning-winking** (gesturing at the depth)

Law 0 generates all of these bans: plain-not-clever, muted-not-vivid, sub+space-not-
wall-of-mid, show-don't-explain, reveal-never-instruct, depth-emerges-never-reached-for.

**Clarification — "never reach" is not "stay flat."** Law 0 bans *performing* or
*faking* depth. It does **not** ban earned emotional peaks. Act 4's cathartic discharge
is a release the journey earned, not a reach. The drop can be enormous. What it must
never be is *unearned* spectacle reaching for a feeling it didn't build. **Don't reach
≠ don't peak.**

The grammar, rendered per medium. This table is the point of the file.

| Law | AUDIO | VISUAL | COPY |

sound layers and visual render-layers, but forcing it onto copy is fake (tested and
rejected 2026-07-21). Copy is organized by voice-register + act, not by the engine.

## THE MIRROR PRINCIPLE

**Every piece is a mirror — valid at two depths at once.** The outsider looking in gets
a true, complete read at the surface ("cool," "this hits"). The insider looking at
themselves gets the reflection underneath (the cosmology, *seen not stated*). **Depth
is rewarded, never required.** One piece, two depths — never two versions for two
audiences. This is WORLD §2's Mirror ("mirrors all the way in") applied to every
surface. Low door, deep room.

**The tightrope (WORLD §0.2) is the engine of this, not a restriction.** Surface stays
plain/psychological/sensory = the outsider's valid read. Cosmology stays submerged =
the insider's reflection. The rule that keeps the world from cheapening is the same
rule that makes it work on both levels.

**The utility-floor exemption.** Not every string is a mirror. Pure-function copy —
logistics ("doors 9pm, $15"), links, EPK facts, press one-sheets — cannot mirror all
the way in, and torturing it into trying is worse than leaving it plain. Utility copy
has one job: **be clean and not break the world** (no woo, no hype-spam, plain). It is
exempt from the mirror principle; it is not exempt from Law 0.

## THE §8 REGISTER-GATE — how deep a surface may reach

WORLD §8 (amended 2026-07-21): **the center may be SHOWN, never EXPLAINED.** The
cosmology may be revealed on any surface in any **abstract/figurative** register; never
in the **plain doctrinal** register (prose stating it as fact/instruction).

Applied across the grammar, each surface has registers at different depths, and the
register gates how far toward the center it may go:
- **Plain register** (light captions, functional copy, a clean wireframe form) — stays
  at the surface. Cosmology submerged.
- **Figurative register** (void poems, the Passage, an abstract focal form rendered as
  the center) — may reach the center, figuratively. Never as doctrine.

Same gate, every surface: **reveal freely in the art-register, never in the
doctrine-register.**
