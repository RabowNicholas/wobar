# WOBAR: OBSCURA
## What It Is
The future version of Wobar made performable. Not a separate alias — the same vessel, different frequency. Where Wobar takes you through the portal, OBSCURA is what exists inside the mirror. Behind the reflection. The gap between the observer and the image looking back.
OBSCURA expands Act 5 Integration into its own complete world. It does not arc horizontally. It builds vertically — not moving somewhere, going deeper into the same place. The void as destination, not passage.
The voice is Sage entirely. Messages from the future self transmitted through the present vessel. Nick as conduit, not author.
---
## Relationship To Wobar
Two modes of the same project. Wobar takes you through. OBSCURA receives you on the other side. Neither is complete without the other — but each stands alone.
They can coexist on the same night as separate experiences. The crowd figures out the relationship themselves. That is part of the transmission.

## Neuroscience Foundation
Built on the convergence of Yoga Nidra and Holotropic States methodology, grounded in brainwave entrainment research.
**Yoga Nidra** provides the four-stage map — a precise movement through consciousness states while maintaining awareness. The practitioner reaches the witness state (pure awareness without content) and returns with integration happening during the return journey.
**Holotropic States** provides the container philosophy — the inner healer is always present and moves toward wholeness when given the right container. The facilitator holds space, does not direct. The healing resource is inside the person, not the facilitator.
**Key neurological mechanism:** Theta state induction through sustained, intentional stimulus. Theta (4–8 Hz) is where memory consolidation, creative insight, and emotional processing occur. The brain is maximally receptive. The walls are thinnest here.
**DMN suppression:** Sustained altered states downregulate the Default Mode Network — responsible for self-referential thought and ego narrative. When the DMN quiets, the boundary between self and experience dissolves. Sub-bass frequencies accelerate DMN downregulation through vagus nerve engagement — physical resonance doing neurological work.
**Movement vs stillness:** Theta state induction does not require stillness. Repetitive rhythmic movement, focused tactile interaction, and stillness all produce the shift. OBSCURA holds all three simultaneously.

The primary entrainment mechanism. Works somatically — through the body via the vagus nerve, not just auditory processing. The floor and chest conduct as much as the speakers.
| Stage | Sub-Bass Range | Quality |
|-------|---------------|---------|
| APERTURE | 40–60 Hz | Soft. Felt in chest. Warmth without aggression. |

| EXPOSURE | Softening | Higher harmonics returning. Warmth replacing depth. |

Embedded as tonal drones underneath the music. Physically present without being announced.
| Stage | Primary Hz | Function |

Single-channel pulsing tones at specific rates. Embedded in the mix. Brain entrains to the pulse rate. Viable on large sound systems.

## The Four Stages
### STAGE 1 — APERTURE
*The opening. Light entering. The hole through which the void begins.*
**Brainwave:** Beta → Alpha
**Minimum duration:** 10–15 minutes
**Solfeggio:** 396 Hz
**Isochronic:** 10–12 Hz
**What is happening**
Surface tension releasing. Heart rate slowing. Cortisol dropping. The nervous system registering safety. The room becoming the container. The crowd arriving into something already alive — no announcement, no beginning they witnessed.
**Musical elements**
- Long evolving pads, minimal harmonic movement
- No percussion or very minimal — gentle pulse only
- Spatial width maximum — sound surrounds rather than hits
- Tempo unanchored or very slow
- Textures breathing, not static
- 396 Hz woven into pad tones
**Staging**
Nick not visible. Music live from behind the haze wall or equivalent membrane. The crowd has no performer to focus on — attention moves to the room, the music, the experience. Invitation implicit: sit, close eyes, breathe. No instruction needed.
**Installations**
Minimal activation. Mirror field alive but subtle. Light slow and ambient.
---
### STAGE 2 — CHAMBER
*Inside the dark room. Full immersion. The void holding you.*
**Brainwave:** Alpha → Theta
**Minimum duration:** 15–20 minutes
**Solfeggio:** 417 Hz → 528 Hz
**Isochronic:** 7–8 Hz
**What is happening**
DMN beginning to quiet. Self-referential thought slowing. The boundary between self and room softening. Memory and emotional processing activating at theta onset. The pull deepening.
**Musical elements**
- Bass patterns emerging — hypnotic, repetitive, evolving slowly
- Rhythm arriving but loose — swing, halftime feel, nothing aggressive
- Harmonic complexity increasing — textures layering
- Spatial movement — elements drifting, depth increasing
- Filter sweeps pulling sound inward
- 528 Hz woven into melodic content
**Staging**
Nick still not present. The void holds without a source.
**Installations**
Mirror field responding to sub-bass. Prisms activating. First interactive panels live. Crowd begins discovering triggers without understanding them.
---
### STAGE 3 — PROJECTION
*The image forming on the interior surface. The future self visible.*
**Brainwave:** Sustained Theta
**Minimum duration:** 15–30 minutes
**Solfeggio:** 852 Hz → 963 Hz
**Isochronic:** 4–6 Hz
**What is happening**
DMN maximally suppressed. Ego boundary dissolved. The witness state — pure awareness without self-referential content. The future self most audible. The holotropic inner healer most active. Maximum receptivity. The deepest point.
**Musical elements**
- Maximum hypnotic repetition — minimal change, maximum depth
- Bass as the primary voice — everything else serves it
- Space between elements — silence as content
- Transmissions arriving — moments of sudden clarity then return to void
- Rhythm dissolved into texture
- 963 Hz at the peak moment
**Staging**
Nick arrives here. No announcement. The haze wall thins or he moves through it. The void shifts when he does. Not a performer arriving — a transmission source becoming present inside a state the crowd is already deep in. He emerges from within it, not from outside.
**Installations**
Fully active. All panels responsive. Mirror field at maximum depth. Personal objects distributed or available. The interaction completing the circuit.
---
### STAGE 4 — EXPOSURE
*The image fixing itself. Integration completing. The experience becoming permanent.*
**Brainwave:** Theta → Alpha → Beta
**Minimum duration:** 15–20 minutes
**Solfeggio:** 639 Hz → 285 Hz
**Isochronic:** 8–12 Hz
**What is happening**
Integration happening in real time. Emotional processing completing. The body remembering itself. Warmth entering. The nervous system returning to regulated baseline. The return journey is where the experience becomes memory and meaning.
**Musical elements**
- Warmth entering harmonic content — minor resolving toward major or suspended
- Rhythm becoming more grounded and clear
- Melodic elements returning — something recognizable after the void
- Tempo stabilizing
- Space still present but filling with warmth rather than void
- 639 Hz woven into melodic resolution
**Staging**
Nick's presence fading back into the room. The return does not need a performer. It needs a container.
**Installations**
Light warming — color temperature shifting. Mirror field slowing. Panels still responsive but gentler.

## Timing Guidelines
Based on Yoga Nidra and neurofeedback research. More strict than the 5-Act framework because the neurological shifts require minimum durations to function.
| Stage | Minimum | Optimal |
|-------|---------|---------|
| APERTURE | 10 min | 15 min |
| CHAMBER | 15 min | 20 min |
| PROJECTION | 15 min | 30 min |
| EXPOSURE | 15 min | 20 min |
| **Total** | **55 min** | **85 min** |
Anything under 45 minutes total does not allow enough time in theta for meaningful DMN suppression and integration. The first 30 minutes is getting there. The work happens after.

## Staging Architecture
**The haze wall** — a laser wall with sufficient haze to create a visible membrane between Nick and the room. Form without definition. Presence without identity. The camera obscura aperture made physical — light passing through a membrane to project inside.
**Large installations** — mirrors, prisms, lasers, lights. Audio-reactive via DMX. The crowd inside the reflection, not in front of it.
**Interactive panels** — freestanding, placed throughout the room. OSC or MIDI signal to Ableton. Each panel represents a different transmission. Crowd discovers interaction intuitively — never instructed.
**Personal objects** — small, engraved with OBSCURA symbolic language. Some remain in the room. Some leave with the person. Eventually become identity totems — recognized across events and scenes.
