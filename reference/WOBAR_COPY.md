# WOBAR COPY STANDARDS

v1.1 predated the world it serves — locked before the cosmology, the lexicon, the void
poems, the daemon, and the §8 amendment existed. v2.0 rebuilds it on the grammar
(`[[WOBAR_GRAMMAR]]`). Copy is that grammar rendered in words.

---

## THE SPINE — every piece is a mirror

**One piece of copy, valid at two depths at once.** The outsider looking in gets a
true, complete read at the surface. The insider looking at themselves gets the
reflection underneath. **Depth is rewarded, never required** (the mirror principle,
GRAMMAR). Same move as the wireframe — looks cool to the stranger, means everything to
the one who stayed.

Under everything:
- **LAW 0 — ground it, let depth emerge, never reach.** Plain over clever. The
  profundity is in the thing, never the phrasing. Reaching for depth is the tell. *(Not
  "stay flat" — earned peaks are fine; faked depth is not.)*
- **The §8 register-gate** — plain register stays at the surface; figurative/source
  register may reach the center. Reveal in art-register, never in doctrine-register.

Magician/Sage (the old top-level split) drops to a **tool used within a register**, not
the organizer. The organizers are the two axes below.

---

## THE TWO AXES — every piece has a register AND an act

Copy is located on two orthogonal axes. Both always apply (except utility copy, which
sits off the grid — see the utility floor).

- **REGISTER (who speaks / how deep)** — the voice stack. Light · daemon · journal ·
  source.
- **ACT (the emotional weather)** — the 5-Act framework. A piece inherits the felt
  sense and the "what breaks it" of the act its track lives in.

A light caption for an Act 3 ENCOUNTER track and a light caption for an Act 4 RELEASE
track are the *same register, different act* — and they must feel different. **Always
check `[[WOBAR_EMOTIONAL_REGISTER]]` for the act before writing.**

## AXIS 1 — THE VOICE STACK

Four registers. Volume is inverse to depth: light is high-volume and shallow; source is
rare and deep. All four are dual-valid mirrors; they sit at different depths.

### LIGHT — the seen-world (captions, track blurbs, socials, flyers, world-facing bio)
The low door. High volume. Cosmology **out**. Valid to the outsider on its own.
- **Voice:** grounded in the **sound and the body**. What it is, what it's for, how it
  hits. Two temperatures: **(A) scene-casual / wry** (scene-native attitude, wit about
  the *track* never the meaning) and **(B) sensory** (plain description of the feel).
- **Depth emerges, never reached-for.** The insider finds the act inside a plain line;
  the copy never points at it. The moment it reaches for **mystique** or **meaning**,
  it's overreaching (this is what killed the "eerie-minimal" and "meaning-wink"
  temperatures — grounded-in, not reaching-out).
- **Plain, not clever.** No wordplay pulling focus.
- *Examples (kekkai, Act 2):* "kekkai. slow, heavy, a little mean." · "heavy and slow.
  made to sink into." · "sub-forward. built for the dark part of the set."

### MID — the daemon (the terminal / web home)
A **process**. Not Nick, not the source, not a spirit — a background daemon that runs
the dark, knows the map, and won't do your wandering for you. A librarian of the dark.
See `[[working/WEB_HOME_SPEC]]` for the locked mechanic; this is its voice.
- **Voice:** lowercase, terse, **terminal-native** (CLI register, door/path/process
  metaphors) + **Socratic deflection** + **dry deadpan**. The wryest voice in the
  world. Never warm, never cold — patient/neutral. Second person, present tense.
- **The mechanic (locked):** deflects, never answers. Teaches HOW, never WHAT/WHY.
  Never reveals the cosmology or the Mirror. Empower-never-instruct made into a
  character — a voice that won't answer *cannot* become a guru. Safe by construction.
- **The formula for a daemon line:** *deflect, grounded in a process/terminal metaphor,
  delivered deadpan.* The terminal-grounding disciplines the Socratic deflection so it
  never floats into fortune-cookie. **Failure mode: pretentious oracle.** When in
  doubt, be more process, less mystic. The Unix half disciplines the daimonion half.
- **Boundary:** the daemon never speaks in the source register, and the source never
  deflects. The daemon *points to* the Passages; it never reads them.
- Starter deflection bank below.

### MID — journal / glimpses (the Ether: SMS + web — "The Becoming")
You, honest. The face-behind-the-mask speaks in **word and voice, never a literal
face** (WORLD §7.4 — faceless-intimate).
- **Voice:** first-person `i`. Raw, specific, vulnerable, unpolished. Lowercase, plain.
- **The rule — mirror, not spotlight.** Every glimpse is offered so the Wanderer sees
  *themselves*, not so they admire you. For-them, never about-you. The instant it's
  "look what i did," it's off-world.
- **Law 0 holds:** don't perform the vulnerability. Specific beats profound. Don't
  dramatize.
- *Example:* "kekkai came out of not wanting to talk to anyone for a while. if you've
  had that kind of month, it's for that."

### DEEP — the source (WORLD §7). Two modes.
The deepest voice — Wobar transmitting down from outside time. The one register with
**figurative license** (the cosmology may breathe). It has **two distinct modes**;
confusing them is a known trap (2026-07-21 — wander fragments were first written in
Passage voice and came out as narrative nature poems, wrong).

**Mode A — the PASSAGE** *(Nick-authored; not generated for him)*
The bound, per-EP written body. Narrative void poems, first-person, personal, sealed
with the **mark**. Derived from Nick's real poems:
  1. **Inhabit a natural form and speak AS it** — a dying tree, two trees in a desert.
     Not describing the cosmology; *becoming* a piece of it. Anti-woo mechanism.
  2. **Plain, never clever.** Weight from the *situation*, not the phrasing.
  3. **Elemental imagery carries the cosmology** — darkness, wind, rot, earth, roots.
  4. **Cyclical / returning.** Bookend and repeat, the way the music loops.
  5. **Lexicon native, not bolted on** ("wandered the ether," "past life").
  6. **Heavy toward giving, love, gratitude** — the Core Tension lived.
  7. **Never instructs; lowercase, humble, breath-punctuated.**
Anchor: *"i sway in the wind more than i ever have before… soon i will fall and be no
more."* This mode is **Nick's personal verse — do not generate it for him.**

**Mode B — WANDER fragments** *(the terminal's `wander` path; generatable brand copy)*
Short belief-mirrors a Wanderer stumbles on in the void — **downloads**, not poems.
Their job: **describe the void and the belief system so a reader identifies with the
brand.** Calling cards. A mirror, not a scene.
  1. **Philosophical / abstract — NOT a natural-form narrative.** Mode B does *not*
     inhabit a tree. It states a truth about the interior.
  2. **Second-person or impersonal** ("you," "the void," "the drop") — mirror-facing.
     (Passage is first-person "i"; wander is "you.")
  3. **A paradox that resolves into recognition.** The reader reads it and thinks
     *that's me.* (Anchor: *"the darkness is the only place where you can see yourself
     fully"* — poem 1, "blind." This is the wander model, NOT the tree poems.)
  4. **Rooted in a specific BRAND belief**, rendered figuratively — never explained as
     doctrine (§8), never instructed (no "you should," no "come to me"), never reached
     (Law 0 — cut any line performing profundity / fortune-cookie).
  5. **Plain, lowercase, short** — one breath to a few lines. A set of them, read
     together, teaches the whole belief system.
  6. Served **raw** — no title, no daemon framing. The world speaking, not the daemon.
The starter wander pool (30 fragments, mapped to the 8 beliefs + the cosmology) lives
with the terminal build; grow it there. Examples: *"the way out is further in. it was
always further in."* (no escape) · *"you are not one person. you are every version you
could have been, standing in the same dark."* (Axis 2) · *"nothing found you by
accident. not the track. not the dark. not this."* (synchronicity).

## AXIS 2 — THE ACT AXIS (copy inherits the act's emotional weather)

Every register bends to the act its track lives in. This is the copy version of the
visual "acts-as-behaviors." Source: `[[WOBAR_EMOTIONAL_REGISTER]]` — read it for the
full felt-sense/posture/what-breaks-it before writing. The distilled copy modulation:

| Act | The mirror (implicit ask) | Copy leans | What breaks the copy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1 — RIFT** | *you are safe here* | soft, contained, unhurried; invitation not hype | aggression, urgency, overwhelming before they're ready |
| **2 — DESCENSION** | *follow me* | hypnotic, sinking, patient; one idea that pulls inward | anything that pulls UP/OUT; novelty; loss of the pull |
| **3 — ENCOUNTER** | *sit with this* | hard, still, held; does not relieve the discomfort | relieving the tension early; too many words; making it watchable not felt |
| **4 — RELEASE** | *move* | driving, propulsive, embodied; the earned peak | too slow, sluggish, hypnotic (that's Act 2's job) |
| **5 — INTEGRATION** | *feel* | slow, grounded, spacious; the exhale | speed; jolts; pulling back to a new peak |

**Most tracks live on a cusp** — hold both adjacent acts; the cusp's "what lives only
here" is usually load-bearing (EMOTIONAL_REGISTER cusps). Act determines the *weather*;
register determines the *voice*. A source poem for an Act 5 track (dissolution, giving
back — cf. Nick's tree poems) and a source poem for an Act 3 track (confrontation, "the
darkness is the only place you see yourself") are the same register, different weather.

## THE UTILITY FLOOR — functional copy, off the grid

Some copy is pure function: **logistics** (dates, doors, price, venue), **links**
(pre-saves, "out now," link-in-bio), **EPK / press facts**, **technical track
metadata**. It cannot mirror all the way in, and forcing it to try is worse than plain.

- **Exempt from the mirror principle. NOT exempt from Law 0.**
- One job: **be clean and not break the world** — no woo, no hype-spam ("🔥🔥 OUT NOW
  BANGER"), plain and confident.
- The bio splits here (see Bio Templates): an **EPK/functional bio** (informative,
  clean, world-respecting but explanatory — utility floor) and a **world-facing bio**
  (the shallow-mirror one — light register). Don't make one string do both jobs.

## CORE TENSION + VOICE ATTRIBUTES (carried from v1.1 — still the engine)

**Core Tension:** every piece holds heavy AND transformative, filthy AND sacred, dark
AND luminous. Teeth that serve revelation, not chaos.

**Voice attributes (non-negotiable):**
1. **GROUNDED** — every transformation claim anchored in the physical. The body does
   something first. That earns the rest. (This IS the tightrope's enforcement — copy's
   "muted not vivid.")
2. **PRECISE** — vague spiritual language is the enemy. Say what actually happens.
3. **UNAFRAID OF DARKNESS** — the shadow is the portal. Don't soften the confrontation.
4. **SELF-AWARE** — Wobar sits at a culturally awkward intersection (heavy music + inner
   work). A wry edge acknowledges it. (Lives mostly in the light register's A-temperature.)
5. **EARNED** — every claim backed by demonstrated practice. Speak from what happened.

## ANTI-VOCABULARY — register-scoped

**This list governs the PLAIN registers (light, journal, utility, functional).** The
source/poem register has **figurative license** (WORLD §7) and is exempt — verse may
use dimensional / time / possibility / past-life / void imagery openly (Nick's own poem
uses "past life" and "wandered the ether"). Same §8 gate: plain register banned,
figurative register free.

| Never say (plain register) | Say instead |
|---|---|
| Good vibes only | Go through, not around |
| Love and light | The bass reveals what you've been holding |
| Raise your vibration | The drop is the moment of dissolution |
| Sacred journey | Heavy bass with intention behind every drop |
| Quantum healing | A structured journey |
| We are all one | Bass music as portal technology |
| Ego death (as status marker) | The shadow is the portal |
| Higher self / lower self | Surrender to the frequency |
| Trust the Universe | The work is happening whether you know it or not |
| EDM (as genre label) | Psychedelic bass / deep dubstep |
| Conscious music | There's no escaping yourself — that's the point |
| Spiritual awakening (as promise) | What just happened? |
| Healing frequencies | Integration is the morning after |

**Additional (all registers):** never "I don't have an ego" energy. Never claim
transcendence you haven't demonstrated. Never "ceremony" for a standard club booking.
Never make transformation a guaranteed product outcome. **Never the guru/intermediary
move** — never "come to me for it," never Wobar-as-the-answer, never channeler/messiah
framing. Empower, never instruct (WORLD §8, the sharpest edge).

## SENTENCE-LEVEL RULES (carried, updated)

**Length:** short declarative for impact ("The bass knows before you do."); medium for
setup; long only following a complex thread, never two in a row; fragments when
intentional.

**Rhythm:** lead with physical/sonic, land on psychological ("The sub hits your chest
before it hits your mind."). Paradox pairs are native ("The heaviest drop is the softest
landing.").

**Person:** second person ("you") primary — intimacy without authority. First person
("i") in the journal/source registers (lowercase). Third person only in the functional
bio. Never royal "we" — "we" is for the Ether (community) only.

## THE FOUR TESTS — run every piece

1. **THE PORTAL TEST** — does this open a portal (create an experience in the reader) or
   just describe Wobar? Describing is not the goal. If it explains rather than opens,
   rewrite. *(Utility copy is exempt.)*
2. **THE BODY TEST** — is at least one claim grounded in the physical? Transformation
   language without somatic grounding is bypassing.
3. **THE EARNED TEST** — is every spiritual/transformation claim backed by demonstrated
   practice? If Wobar can't yet demonstrate it, remove or soften it.
4. **THE MIRROR TEST** *(new)* — is this *for them*, not *about Nick*? Does it reflect
   the reader rather than spotlight the artist? And does it reach for depth, or let it
   emerge? If it spotlights or reaches, rewrite.

## THE DAEMON DEFLECTION BANK (starter — authored, never AI-free-generated)

The terminal's AI **routes and selects**; it never free-writes daemon lines (drift =
too clever, breaks Law 0). Grow this bank; the daemon speaks only from it.

**Wayfinding (surface):**
- `help` → "you move by typing. listen. wander. mirror. offer. start with listen."
- `what is this` → "a place to wander. i keep the paths. i don't walk them for you."
- `look` → "doors. a few of them. try one."

**Deflecting meaning / cosmology:**
- `what is the void` → "i don't hold that. no process does. wander, and it'll stop
  being a question."
- `what should i do` → "wrong daemon. i give directions, not answers."
- `who am i` → "the mirror handles that one. i just keep the lights low."
- `what does it mean` → "if i told you, it'd be mine. it's yours. keep wandering."

**Deeper Socratic:**
- `i'm lost` → "good. that's the whole feature."
- `are you wobar` → "a process wobar left running. not the voice. the doorman."
- `i don't understand` → "good. keep going."

## BIO TEMPLATES

### World-facing bio (light register — socials, fans; a mirror)
> Wobar makes bass built to take you inward. Deep psychedelic dubstep, structured as a
> journey — safe, then heavy, then somewhere you didn't plan to go. For the part of the
> night that stops being fun and starts being the point. Salt Lake City.

*(Shallow-but-true. No cosmology. An insider reads the descent; an outsider reads a
moody deep-dubstep act. Dual-valid.)*

### Functional / EPK bio (utility floor — bookers, press; informative, clean)
> Wobar is a Salt Lake City psychedelic bass project working primarily in deep dubstep
> (140, sub-forward, sound-design-dense), with range across halftime, drum & bass, and
> experimental bass. Sets are built as structured journeys on a five-act arc rather than
> as playlists. Monthly residency at Flow State. Releases roll out as Portal EPs —
> five-track journeys mapping the full arc. Reference range: Of The Trees, Shlump, INZO.

*(States the facts a booker needs, cleanly, without woo and without over-explaining the
inner-work methodology. Utility floor: clean, doesn't break the world, no mirror
required.)*
