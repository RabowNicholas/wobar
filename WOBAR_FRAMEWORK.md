# 5-ACT PORTAL FRAMEWORK

A therapeutic bass music structure mapping electronic music performance to authentic shadow work methodology.

**Operating principle:** Operates at the journey level — sets, Portal EPs — not individual tracks. Use as filter after creative flow, not constraint during production. Most tracks live on the cusp of two acts. This is a feature, not a problem.

---

## ACT 1: RIFT — Container Creation
**Emotional register:** "I am safe here. I can explore. Something is beginning."

- Warm, inviting pads and melodies
- Gentle sub bass (felt, not aggressive)
- INZO/LSDREAM opening energy — mystical, welcoming
- Minimal drums, building gradually
- BPM: Flexible, often starting slower/ambient
- Visual: Circles dominate. Warm purple glow. Minimal movement.

---

## ACT 2: DESCENSION — Journey Into Shadow
**Emotional register:** "I'm being pulled somewhere. There's something I need to see."

- Psychedelic bass — Of The Trees, Tipper
- Dynamic movement — evolving textures and builds, not static groove
- Rolling sub bass with shifting patterns
- BPM: 128–142
- Stereo width, spatial depth, evolving filter sweeps
- Visual: Circles begin spiraling. Colors deepen. Pull toward center intensifies.

---

## ACT 3: ENCOUNTER — Confrontation / Recognition
**Emotional register:** "What is this? That's MY shadow. Oh fuck, I see it now."

- Heavy experimental bass — Herbalistek, Kursa, G Jones
- Dissonant, unsettling, weird textures
- Unpredictable — breaks pattern expectations
- NO emotional relief, NO vocals
- Confrontational heaviness — pushes you into the ground
- Visual: Spirals become tunnels. Mirror distortion at maximum. No warm colors.

**Key distinction from Act 4:** Act 3 is confrontational and oppressive — psychological heaviness, makes people sway and feel pushed into the ground. Act 4 is cathartic release. Do not conflate these.

---

## ACT 4: RELEASE — Cathartic Discharge
**Emotional register:** "I need to MOVE this. Get it OUT. YES. FUCK YES."

- Phase 1 (30–40%): Physical Discharge — Heavy halftime, swing drums, 808s
- Phase 2 (40–50%): Emotional Release — Vocals, melodic support, INZO/LSDREAM energy
- Phase 3 (20–30%): Peak Catharsis — Multiple drops (2–4), heavy but relieving
- Visual: Explosion of movement and color. Full palette activated. Heavy but held.

---

## ACT 5: INTEGRATION + EMBODIMENT — Grounded Return
**Emotional register:** "What does this mean? I understand. I'm back. I'm safe. I'm complete."

- Integration (30%): Melodic pads, minor→major shifts, processing space
- Embodiment (40%): Gentle bass, callbacks to Act 1 themes
- Grounding (30%): Portal closing, circular narrative complete
- Visual: Return to circles. Colors fade back to purple and black. Motion slows.

## Percentage Guidelines

### Standard Set
| Act | Percentage |
|-----|-----------|
| Act 1: RIFT | 10% |
| Act 2: DESCENSION | 20% |
| Act 3: ENCOUNTER | 15% |
| Act 4: RELEASE | 35% |
| Act 5: INTEGRATION + EMBODIMENT | 20% |

### Double Journey Set (2–3 Hours)
| Segment | Percentage |
|---------|-----------|
| Act 1 | 10% |
| Act 2 (first cycle) | 15% |
| Act 3 (first layer) | 10% |
| Act 4 (first catharsis) | 20% |
| Act 2 (reprise) | 10% |
| Act 3 (deeper) | 10% |
| Act 4 (peak catharsis) | 15% |
| Act 5 (extended) | 20% |

---

## Non-Negotiables
- Always start with Act 1
- Acts follow in order — no random jumping
- Act 4 requires enough time for full catharsis
- Never skip integration entirely
